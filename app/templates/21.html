Inspiration through Philosophy: 21.January

"It would be interesting to inquire
how many times essential advances in science
have first been made possible by the fact
that the boundaries of special disciplines
were not respected...
Trespassing is one of the most
successful techniques in science."

Wolfgang Köhler
(21.01.1887 - 11.06.1967)


A German psychologist and phenomenologist
who contributed to the creation
of Gestalt psychology.
