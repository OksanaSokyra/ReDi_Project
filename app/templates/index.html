Inspiration through Philosophy: January

Right words for every day

Sometimes we all need a small piece of inspiration.

And where can we find more inspiration
than within the philosophy?


This project was designed to light up every day
with a short phrase
and make it a little bit more inspiring.
The choice of quotes is based on the birthdate
or date of death of the philosophers.

So, pick up any date from 01.01 to 31.01
to see which philosopher was born or died that day,
and what was one of his/her´s most wise words.
